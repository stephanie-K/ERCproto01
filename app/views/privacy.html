Privacy notice

East Renfrewshire Council is the 'data controller' of the personal information you give us and we collect and use about you. This covers personal information of both council staff and members of the public.
We generally use information about you to provide public services and carry out our statutory functions.

We may collect information by:

  • paper
  • online form
  • telephone
  • email
  • CCTV
  • a member of our staff
  • an external agency acting on our behalf

This notice explains why we collect your information and what we use it for. It also gives more detail how different Council services use and share your information.

Contact us

You can contact us on 0141 577 3000.

For data protection issues, email the Council's Data Protection officer at: dpo@eastrenfrewshire.gov.uk

If you're concerned about how we use your personal data, please first contact the Council's Data Protection Officer.

If you're still unhappy, you can contact the Information Commissioner at:
