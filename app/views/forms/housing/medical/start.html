Medical Priority Application

If you are applying for Medical Priority for more than one person, a form must be completed for each applicant.

Before you start

You need to have (if applicable):

  • your Housing application number
  • the name and dosage of medications
  • Disability benefit details
  • the name and address of doctors and any consultant or clinic attended
