Apply online for primary 1 - August 2019

This a 2 stage process. You must apply online by 11 January 2019. We’ll then ask you to take proof of your details to the school between 14 and 18 January 2019.

Step 1: Apply online

Before you start

  • you should read the admissions policy
  • you can only apply to your catchment school
  • you still need to apply to your catchment school if you make a placing request, later move house or defer entry
  • closing date is 11 January 2019 at 4pm

Step 2: Provide proof of details

Date: From 14 to 18 January 2019

Time: Between 1.45pm and 3.30pm

Place: School office

You don’t need to make an appointment.

Documents you need to bring

These must be original documents. School office staff will take a copy and return them.

  • child's birth certificate
  • current Council Tax notice
  • Roman Catholic baptism certificate (if applying to a Roman Catholic school)
  • mortgage statement or rent agreement covering the last year
  • Child Benefit statement
  • if you don’t get Child Benefit, you can bring a Driving licence, utility bill or bank statement

When you’ll hear from us

We’ll write to you by 31 March 2019.
