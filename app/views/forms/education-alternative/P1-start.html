This service is for Primary 1 enrolment only.

Before you start

Check this is the right service. You need to:

  • have parental rights and responsibilities for the P1 child
  • live in East Renfrewshire
  • want to enrol at your catchment school

You can apply to enrol even if you're:

  • applying to defer primary 1 entry for your child
  • moving house later and need to apply to another school

For other schools, you need to apply for a placing request.
